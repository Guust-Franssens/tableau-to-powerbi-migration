# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want participation in this project to be a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and considerate in issues, pull requests, and reviews.
- Assume good faith, and critique ideas and code rather than people.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully, and give it kindly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, insults, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sharing customer or otherwise confidential data (see [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md)).
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests, and any related
communication — and when representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the maintainer (see [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) for private
contact channels). Reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially and in good faith.
